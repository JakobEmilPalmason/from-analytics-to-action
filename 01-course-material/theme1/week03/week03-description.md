### Week 3 (16 Feb) - The value of data

Based on an extensive literature review Xu et al. study the changing role and value of data in organisations. They argue that the role of data has evolved beyond its traditional use as an informational resource and propose a new typology, categorsing data into four distinct roles: 1) data as tool, 2) data as commodity, 3) data as a practice, and 4) data as algorithmic intelligence. The authors suggest that organisations should recognise and adapt to these different roles to fully leverage data’s potential. Moreover, they suggest that the way data is conceptualized directly influences how businesses extract value from it, affecting decision-making, market strategies, and competitive advantages.
